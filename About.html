About The Cavaliers

The Calvary Day School Soccer Team was established in 1961. Over the years there has been
a plethora of great soccer players, but many have said this may be the best team they have seen
throughout the years. This may be due the the great work ethic of the player. These players are
seen working hard on and off the field. They put in the work for an hour and a half on a day to
day basis.

The two great coaches are also to thank. Coach Reigner and his assitant, Coach Justin, help
the boys through everthing. These coaches push the boys over their limits making them
better players. The players and the fans love how hard the coaches work to give them
an advantage on the field.

Then we have the fans.The fans are so amazing, they, encourage and cheer on the players.
They always stick with us through the good times and bad. These three aspects of the
coaches, the fans, and the players, are what make us such a great team.
See you on the pitch for this new 2023 season.
